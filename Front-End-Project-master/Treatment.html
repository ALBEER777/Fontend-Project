Self Care

If you have mild Symptoms, stay at home until you have recovered. you can relieve your Symptomsif you:

Rest and Sleep

Keep Warm

Drink plenty of liquids

Use a room humidifier or take a hot shower to help ease a store throat and cough

Medical Treatments

If you develop a fever, cough, and have difficulty breathing, promptly seek medical care. call in advance and tell your health provider of any recent travel or recent contact with travelers.

BE CAREFULL & STAY SAFE

TREATMENT FOR CORONAVIRUS

To date, there is no vaccine and no specific antiviral medicine to prevent or treat COVID-19.However,those affected should receive care to relieve symptoms. people with serious illness should be hospilalized. Most patients recover thanks to supportive care.

Antibiotics do not help, as they do not work against viruses. Treatment aims to relieve the symptoms while your body fights the illness. you will need to stay in isolation, away from other people, until you have recovered.
